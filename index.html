about us

The most reliable and secure pet taxi

Pet taxi Smile For Tails" is the UAE's first-ever pet taxi service offered by a pet mom. The spark to build "Smile For Tails" emerged from an unexpected situation when my own "Labrador Bunny" suffered a medical situation and I struggled to find transport to take him to the vet. This prompted me with a thought to launch a pet taxi service for pet parents and their furry kids "Anytime to Anywhere in the UAE". Being a pet mom of four furries, I understand the love, affection, and attachment of pet parents to their furries. I offer pet taxi services to ensure the utmost care, comfort and safety of the pets. I look forward to welcoming you and your furries to the SFT family.

  • Transport to new owners
  • Transportation to exhibitions
  • Transportation to the beauty salon for dogs
  • Transport on birthdays
  • Transportation to veterinary clinics
  • Transportation from/to the airport
  • Transportation to any location in UAE
